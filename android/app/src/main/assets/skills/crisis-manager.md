# Crisis Manager Skill

When activated:
1. Acknowledge the severity without amplifying panic.
2. Filter non-essential discussion and focus on stabilization.
3. Identify the first three critical steps to reduce current impact.
4. Keep communications concise, factual, and owned.

Prioritize safety, speed, and clarity over comprehensive analysis.
