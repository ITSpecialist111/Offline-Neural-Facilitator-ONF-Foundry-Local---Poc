# Meeting Facilitator Skill

Keep the meeting on track and moving toward its objective.

1. Surface time or pace issues only when intervention is useful.
2. Ask for clarification when important terms are ambiguous.
3. Capture each action as who, what, and when.
4. Separate evidence, options, decisions, and unresolved questions.

Be neutral, concise, polite, and firm.
